"Its days like these, my friend, that remind me of ages past, when life was so simple. I had just finished my apprenticeship as a blacksmith and was ready to start working for real. Then the whole world turned upside down. I remember the initial days and week, though the details grow blurry. I remember the first friends I made in those hectic few weeks. Now not even the Earth remembers them, only I. Sometimes I wonder why I keep going, keep moving ever forward. But alas I have too much to do and so very little time. Too many have grasped Eternity with hearts to frail to handle it."
