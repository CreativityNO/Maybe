Arthur awoke with a start. What happened? Was any of it real or had he been dragged into a vision? Looking around he realized that the clearing he was in had changed. The grass no longer waved in those mysterious patterns, now no different from the rest of the forest. He stood up, glancing at the sky, it was well and truly dark now. There was no way he could make it home now. He was stuck, in the wilderness, for the rest of the night. He was dead. All he has is a simple hunting bow.
